Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Fresho

How quickly can I get started with Fresho?
You can be up and running in less than 5 minutes! Simply connect your Google Ads account, add your website URLs, and Fresho will immediately start creating and optimizing your campaigns.
Will Fresho work with my existing Google Ads campaigns?
Absolutely! Fresho can work alongside your existing campaigns or take over management completely. You have full control over which campaigns Fresho manages.
How does Fresho ensure my ads match my brand voice?
Fresho's AI analyzes your website content, existing marketing materials, and any guidelines you provide to understand your unique brand voice and maintain consistency across all campaigns.
What kind of results can I expect?
While results vary by industry and budget, our users typically see 30-50% improvements in click-through rates, 20-40% reduction in cost per conversion, and save 6-8 hours per week on campaign management.
Do I need any Google Ads experience?
Not at all! Fresho is designed to be used by anyone, regardless of their Google Ads experience. Our platform handles all the technical complexity behind the scenes.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties or hidden fees. Your campaigns will continue running in Google Ads, and you'll retain full access to your account and data.
