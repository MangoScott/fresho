It started with
a nonprofit

Scott founded FinMango in 2016. A nonprofit focused on solving complex problems that endanger the financial health of young adults. 1M+ reached. International acclaim.

Then came the Google Ads problem.

FinMango needed to build awareness. Google offered $10,000/month in free ads. But managing them? Hours every week. Constant optimization. Technical complexity.

Sound familiar? Most nonprofits never use their Grant. Scott reached out to his friend to build an internal solution to automate it.

That solution worked. Really worked.

Fresho was born. Automation that creates, publishes, and optimizes campaigns 24/7.

The advertising landscape is changing

Major browsers eliminated third-party cookies in 2024. Privacy matters. The old targeting methods are gone. New approaches like Google's Privacy Sandbox and aggregated data analysis emerged. Fresho solves this with hyper-specific ads and dynamic adaptation to trends. No cookies required.
