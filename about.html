Our Story

For over a decade, we've been delivering innovative solutions to the world. Solutions that have been featured in top media outlets like CNN and The New York Times. At the heart of our success is a bond of friendship and a shared commitment to making a positive impact in the lives of others.
Early 2010's
Met and became friends in college. Oscar's computer science professor was Scott's dad, James Glasgow, PhD.
Mid to Late 2010's
We built our first side project together in 2016. It was called Worth1k and was used to recycle old tablets to be used as digital frames for your instagram posts. We then built EZ Workout, a Personalized Workout Generation App and co-launched FinMango, a global financial health non-profit that has impacted millions in 90+ countries.
Early 2020's
Co-authored a top trending Op-Ed for U.S. News and World Report. Got our work featured in dozens of media outlets ranging from The New York Times to NBC. Built Raging Robot Radio, an AI-powered radio that we use on a daily basis.
Today
Building Fresho! A revolutionary AI-powered Google Ads platform that helps businesses turn URLs into high-performing ads in seconds.
