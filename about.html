It started with
a nonprofit

Scott founded FinMango in 2016. A nonprofit teaching financial health to young adults across five states. 50,000+ students reached. International acclaim. Featured in CNN and The New York Times.

Then came the Google Ads problem.

FinMango needed to reach students, donors, and volunteers. Google offered nonprofits $10,000/month in free ads. But managing them? Hours every week. Constant optimization. Technical complexity. Resources FinMango didn't have.

Sound familiar? Most nonprofits never use their Google Ad Grant. Too complicated. Too time-consuming. Scott built an internal solution to automate it.

That solution worked. Really worked.

Then the realization hit: thousands of businesses face the exact same problem. They need Google Ads to grow. But they lack the time, expertise, and resources to do it right.

Fresho was born. Automation that creates, publishes, and optimizes campaigns 24/7. From URL to running ads in under 5 minutes. No manual setup. No endless tweaking.

The advertising landscape is changing

Major browsers eliminated third-party cookies in 2024. Privacy matters. The old targeting methods are gone. New approaches like Google's Privacy Sandbox and aggregated data analysis emerged. Fresho solves this with hyper-specific ads and dynamic adaptation to trends. No cookies required.
